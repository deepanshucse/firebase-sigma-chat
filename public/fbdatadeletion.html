Facebook Data Deletion Instructions

Updated on May 06, 2023 By Deepanshu

Sigma chat use Facebook Login to get access to your profile data. This data is used to create account. We do not store data you shared with Facebook app on our servers. All data is stored in Firebase.

According to the Facebook Platform rules, we have to provide a User Data Deletion Callback URL or a Data Deletion Instructions URL.

If you want to delete your activities for Slider Revolution or Essential Grid, you can remove your activities by the following instructions:

  • Go to your Facebook account’s “Setting & Privacy” page. Click ”Setting“.
  • Then, go to Apps and Websites” and you will see all of the apps and websites you linked with your Facebook.
  • Search and click "Sigma chat" in the search bar.
  • Click the ”Remove” button.
  • Congratulations, you have successfully removed your app activities.
